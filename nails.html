Daily Nail Care

Keep Your Nails Looking Fresh Every Day

Following a few simple habits can dramatically increase the lifespan of your manicure while keeping your natural nails healthy and strong.

01

Moisturise Every Day

Apply cuticle oil or hand cream daily to prevent dryness, cracked cuticles and brittle nails. Healthy skin keeps your manicure looking luxurious.

02

Wear Gloves

Protect your nails while washing dishes, cleaning or gardening. Chemicals and excessive water exposure can weaken both natural and artificial nails.

03

Don't Use Nails As Tools

Avoid opening cans, scratching surfaces or lifting objects with your nails. This helps prevent lifting, cracks and breakages.

04

Avoid Biting Or Picking

Picking at gel or acrylic can damage your natural nails. Always have enhancements professionally removed or maintained.

05

Keep Nails Clean

Gently clean underneath your nails using a soft brush. Good hygiene keeps your nails healthy and looking beautiful.

06

Book Regular Maintenance

Regular fills and maintenance appointments help your manicure stay balanced, neat and long lasting.

Healthy Nail Habits

Simple Things That Make A Big Difference

Small daily habits can extend the life of your manicure while keeping your natural nails healthy underneath.

✦

Use Cuticle Oil

Hydrated cuticles improve nail flexibility and reduce lifting around the edges.

♡

Stay Hydrated

Drinking enough water helps support healthy skin, stronger nails and overall nail growth.

✿

Eat A Balanced Diet

Vitamins, minerals and protein contribute to naturally stronger, healthier nails.

★

Protect Against Heat

Excessive heat can affect certain nail products, so avoid prolonged exposure where possible.

❀

Never Peel Products Off

Removing gel or acrylic yourself can damage the nail plate and weaken natural nails.

☾

Visit Your Nail Technician

Professional maintenance ensures your nails remain beautiful, healthy and long lasting.

After Your Appointment

Your First 48 Hours Matter Most

The first couple of days after your appointment are important for keeping your manicure looking flawless for as long as possible.

07

Avoid Excessive Water

Long periods in hot water can soften nails and weaken the bond of certain nail products. Keep exposure to a minimum where possible.

08

Be Gentle

Avoid placing unnecessary pressure on your nails while they settle into everyday wear. Small habits make a big difference.

09

Apply Hand Cream

Healthy hands complement beautiful nails. Moisturise regularly to keep your skin soft and your manicure looking luxurious.

Nail Care Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to some of the most common questions about caring for your nails between appointments.

How often should I book a fill?

+

Most clients return every 2 to 4 weeks depending on nail growth, lifestyle and the service they received.

What should I do if a nail breaks?

+

Avoid pulling or removing the damaged nail yourself. Contact Sihle Nails as soon as possible so we can repair it professionally.

Can I remove my gel or acrylic nails at home?

+

We don't recommend it. Professional removal protects your natural nails and helps prevent unnecessary damage.

Which products should I use at home?

+

A nourishing cuticle oil, quality hand cream and gentle nail brush are all excellent additions to your daily routine.
